# Product Overview

The Custard Screams is a band marketing website for a London-based Post-Hardcore Punk Rock band.

## Purpose

Single-page application showcasing:
- Band information and biography
- Live show listings (upcoming and past)
- Music streaming and downloads
- Social media integration

## Key Features

- Smooth scrolling single-page navigation with section-based routing
- GDPR-compliant cookie consent management with granular controls
- Bandsintown widget integration for live show tracking
- Google Analytics integration (consent-gated)
- Responsive design with punk rock aesthetic
- Accessibility-focused implementation

## Target Audience

Fans, venue bookers, and music industry professionals looking for band information, show dates, and music.

## Brand Identity

Dark theme (black background, white text) with punk rock typography using Bebas Neue and custom punk-style icons.
